Introduction to GroupBuilder

GroupBuilder is online software, also known as "Software as a Service". GroupBuilder enables your organization to more easily and quickly do the things that it does. GroupBuilder was built upon the philosophy of Don't just manage, grow!. Often identified by having "Management System" as the last two words of its name, traditional "management" software often times is little more than glorified contact list management. GroupBuilder was built with a different idea in mind. It was built upon the idea that your organization will flourish if it is easier/faster to perform the duties and activities that naturally occur as part of belonging to your organization.


Core Concepts

Not suprisingly, GroupBuilder is organized around groups and subdivisions of groups organized around time, place and activity. Some of the most important concepts in GroupBuilder center around the following elements:

  • Clubs
  • Members
  • Meetings
  • Roles
  • Agendas
  • Leaders
  • Distribution Lists
  • Absences
